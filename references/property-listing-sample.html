Full description

LETTING SOLUTIONS' OFFER........NOW LET

Stunning three-bedroom maisonette property in a quiet residential part of South Queensferry.
This bright apartment comprises of a welcoming hall, spacious lounge with dining area, large kitchen including oven/cooker, fridge/freezer and washing machine.
One downstairs double bedroom with storage cupboard.
Downstairs also benefits from a WC and more storage.
Upstairs comprises of two double bedrooms, one with an en-suite with shower cubicle.
The large family bathroom has a white three-piece suite and shower over the bath.
High ceilings, tastefully decorated in neutral colours and with cream carpets in living and bedroom areas.
There is ample parking within the estate.

Please note that the pictured furniture does not come with this property. White goods only.

We would highly recommend you call our office as soon as possible if this property is of interest to yourself because this wont be available for long. 01506 425 380.

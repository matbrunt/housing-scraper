Stunningly presented 3 bedroom new build FURNISHED flat set in exclusive development beside the Pentland Hills with large South facing private balcony overlooking the Pentland Hills.

The property boasts 2 large double bedrooms (master with ensuite shower), 1 single bedroom, huge hallway with ample storage facilities, newly fitted shower room, large open plan lounge with dining area and fully fitted kitchen just off. The property has been decorated and furnished to the highest of standards and must be viewed early.
